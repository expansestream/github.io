Powering Up - Powering Down

  • On the central console:
    • Press the top power button.
    • Press the lower power button.
  • On the left console:
    • Press the power button on the Wirecast Gear rack.

No audio on livestream

  • Confirm with sound engineer that the Stream volume is on.
  • In Wirecast, go to Layer 5 and click the microphone icon .
  • Green bars should appear in the Stream Preview window, going up and down.
  • Switch the preview to Live Video.

Cameras Not Loading

  • If any of the cameras do not display, all of the cameras need to be restarted at once.
  • Turn the camera controller off and then on.
  • Press the System button on the camera controller.
    • Under the Power heading, turn the F1 knob until you see ALL OFF is displayed.
    • Push the knob in.
    • All cameras will turn off and Wirecast will display blank feeds in the camera layer.
    • The controller will then display ALL ON.
    • Press the F1 knob again.
    • Repeat the process until all cameras are operational.
  • Cameras will reboot. It may take a few moments before the cameras load in Wirecast.
  • If any camera is showing a blury image, try toggling between presets.
    • Also, try manually adjusting the focus using the large focus knob to the left of the control stick.

Problems Copying Clip-ID

  • Double click on string of numbers.
  • Copy everything that is highlighted.
  • Press the Microsoft key on the keyboard and then the R (run) key.
  • Type notepad into the dialog box.
  • Click OK.
  • Once the notepad app opens, paste the previously copied items.
  • In notepad, copy only the string of numbers.
  • Paste into Video URL described in Step 10.

Power to Dagwell Soundboard

  • Proceed to the back of the sound board.
  • Find the power cord.
  • Locate power switch above power cord.
    • Toggle switch to turn power on/off.

Arrival & Wirecast Setup

Step 1

  • If the computer is not on, boot it up !
  • Log in using the pin: 135013.
  • Open Wirecast.
  • Go to File > Open Recent.
  • Click the appropriate service document:
    • 10:30 am service: /Choral Eucharist.wcst
    • Wilderness: /The Wilderness_backup.wcst
    • Dean's Forum: /Dean's Forum.wcst
    • Weddings, funerals, special services: /Choral Eucharist.wcst

Updating the Service Document

Step 2

  • Click on the title overlay in the first layer labeled Sermon.
  • Click Preacher.
  • Click on the Layer Settings button (the 3 dots in the upper right-hand corner).
  • In the Text field, update preacher as their name appears in the service leaflet.
  • Adjust text field if necessary.

Step 3

  • In the title overlay, click the large plus sign (+).
  • In the window that opens, go to Media Files > Browse Disk.
  • Open the Pictures folder (if not found, open the Downloads folder).
  • The thumbnails are arranged by month, and then by platform.
  • Look at the various thumbnails. Each service has two thumbnails, one for Wirecast and one for Vimeo.
  • Open the Wirecast folder.
  • Select the file for today's service:
  • Choral Eucharist: CE_[date]
  • Wilderness: WI_[date]
  • Dean's Forum: Thumbnails in the Pictures folder.
  • Special Services: Have their own thumbnails.

Setting Up the Cameras

Step 4

  • Turn on the camera controller by pressing the switch located in its back left corner.
  • The camera signals will load in Wirecast once the controller boots up.
  • Press the PMEM/TMEM button to load the camera presets.

Connecting to Vimeo

Step 5

  • Open Google Chrome.
  • Click Vimeo in the Bookmarks bar.
  • Click the large blue button New.
  • Select Host Event from the dropdown menu.
  • In the dialogue box that appears, click Choose event type.
  • In the dialog box that appears, confirm that Live broadcast and Stream with RTMP are selected.
  • Click Next.
  • In the dialogue box that appears, complete the Title field as follows:
    • For the 10:30 am service: Choral Eucharist, Rite II, [date of service]
    • For the Wilderness: The Wilderness, [date of service]
    • For the Dean's Forum: Dean's Forum with [Guest Name], [date]
    • For other services: Name of Service, [date of service]
  • Complete the Start and End fields.
    • Enter the date as well as the start and end times of the service.
    • Do Not Skip This Step! It creates a countdown to let viewers know when the service starts.
  • Verify that Link privacy is set to Public.
  • Click Create.

Step 6

  • A new dialogue box will appear.
  • Click the Settings button in the upper right-hand corner .
  • A dropdown menu will appear.
  • Adjacent to the Vimeo heading, click the Customize button.
  • On the new page that opens, scroll down to Thumbnail.
  • Click the pencil icon .
  • Click Manage settings.
  • In the new dialog box that appears, click Upload.
  • Open the Pictures folder and then the current month’s folder (if not found, open the Downloads folder).
  • Select the appropriate thumbnail:
    • Choral Eucharist: T_CE_[date]
    • Wilderness: T_WI_[date]
    • Dean's Forum: See correct thumbnail.
    • Special Services: See correct thumbnail.
  • Save, and you will see the preview change with the updated thumbnail.
    • If streaming a Wedding, Funeral or Special Event, DISREGARD the following “Destinations Discussion”:
  • Return to the dropdown menu and go to Destinations.
    • Scroll down to Facebook and YouTube, selecting the Saint John’s account for each.
    • Load each account.
    • Do not load using any of the personal accounts.

Step 7

  • Finally, click the link icon in the upper right-hand corner.
  • Copy the link, paste it into a new tab, and open it.
  • Press the D key.
  • In the small box that opens, copy the numeric Clip ID.
  • Keep this tab and the other Vimeo tab open.

Updating the Saint John's Homepage

Step 8

  • Open a new tab and click the SJC WP Admin bookmark.
  • Login to the SJCathedral.org page using the provided credentials.
    • If streaming a Wedding, Funeral or Special Event, do the following:
      • Scroll down the left side and select the Pages tab.
      • a. Weddings are embedded on the Wedding Service page: https://sjcathedral.org/wedding-service/
      • b. Funerals are embedded on the Funeral Service page: https://sjcathedral.org/funeral-service/
      • c. Special services are embedded on the Special Event page: https://sjcathedral.org/special-event/
      • Select the appropriate page and click Edit.
        • On the page that loads, locate text box labled Video URL.
        • Paste the copied Clip ID at the end of the string: https://vimeo.com/
        • Click the blue button Update.
    • In the upper left corner under Saint John’s Cathedral, select Visit Site.
    • On the page that loads, click Edit Page in the top menu bar.
    • Scroll down to intro Settings.
    • Locate text box labled Video URL.
    • Paste the copied Clip ID at the end of the string: https://vimeo.com/
    • Click the blue button Update.

    Starting the Stream

    Step 9


    • About 10 minutes before the service begins, go back to the Wirecast screen.
    • In the topmost toolbar, open the Output Settings window by going to Output > Output Settings (or press Ctrl + Y).
    • On the page that loads, click STREAM TO VIMEO.
    • The RECORDING preset is ’ed.
    • Go back to the opened Vimeo tab. Connect encoder to begin stream prerview will be in the middle of the screen.
    • Copy the RTMPS URL and paste it into the Address: box in the Wirecast Output Settings Window.
    • Copy the Stream key and paste it into the Stream: box in the Wirecast Output Settings Window.
    • Click OK.
    • Click the Record button. It will turn green Record.
    • Click the Stream button. It will turn green Stream.
    • The Stream Is Now Live!

    Ending the Stream

    Step 10


    • Once the service is over, click the Closing Screen overlay and leave it up for a few seconds.
    • End the broadcast by clicking the Stream and Record buttons.
    • Close the Wirecast window by clicking X in the upper right-hand corner.
    • Click Save or Don't Save (generally click Don't Save if new additions like thumbnails don't need to be saved).
    • Close Wirecast. Vimeo will automatically end the broadcast.
    • Close Google Chrome, and shut down the computer.
    • You're done!
